Class Subjects

The one where you will learn how to always show and speak kindness. These seems easy enough but sometimes it is hard when you just don't feel like it. This will help you to understand and look for ways to 'Grow Kindness' in even the hardest situations.
The one where you learn to continue to pursue your dreams regardless of how long it may take or what obstacles you may encounter on its path. This will provide the tools to help you grab on to what you want and continue to reach for that achievement.
The one where you learn that it is OK to start over at any age. Whether you need to pursue a new career or you just need to start a new chapter in your life, this will give you the tools needed to reset and regroup no matter what stage of life you are at.
The one where you will learn how to make money while balancing your life to where you are happy with both. This will halp you find what you need to determine what is the best balance for you. We help you find a way you can have both without sacrificing one.
The one where you will learn how to balance the various portions in your life to where you are sacrificing the least. This will emphasize the importance of not mixing business with pleasure. It also provides the tools you need to determine how to balance career and family.
The one where you learn that it is freeing to just kick back and play guitar and sing. Regardless if you are a beginner or a seasoned musician, this will help you discover that you will get the peace and freedom to express yourself through music.
