Class Subjects

Study Different Dog Breeds

These courses provide tools and knowledge for handling different dog breeds. It covers how to choose the right dog, differences in various breeds and crossbreeds. It will also touch on behavior and socialization of various breeds and how they may differ.
Class Schedule

Dog Training Methods and Rewards

These courses consist of an in-depth Behavior and Obedience training. The goal is to provide tools for hands-on, cruelty-free positive-reinforcement and reward training of various different breeds of dogs. We work with area shelters that will provide the dogs for this training. .
Class Schedule

Dog Body Language

These courses will provide the tools and knowledge needed to read and understand a dog's body language and how to respond safely and appropriately. It will also cover how dogs can be trained to use their body language to effectively communicate with their human companion.
Class Schedule

Dog Nutrition Breakdown

These courses will provide an overview of Nutrigenomics, learn what to feed your dog and why, how various health condition and age of your dog affects their nutrition needs, how to deal with food intolerances and what to feed your dog to hep control weight.
Class Schedule

Concepts of Dog Behavior

These courses will cover various situations regarding dog behavior. It will provide tools and knowledge needed to help solve problem unwanted behavior, successful housetraining, separation anxiety, compulsive behaviors and aggressive behavior with dogs.
Class Schedule

Dog Safety and CPR

These courses will provide the tools and knowledge needed to respond appropriately to a dog's trauma and injury, including checking their vital signs, providing first aid and performing CPR when needed. It will also cover the various predisposed ailments in certain breeds.
Class Schedule
