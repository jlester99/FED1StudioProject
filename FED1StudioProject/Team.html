Meet Our Amazing Team

Leadership

Stella Thompson - Co-Founder/President

Stella co-founded this Academy in late 2019, along with Alexandria Prince. She is very passionate about dogs and her wish is that every dog can be loved and appreciated! She, along with her husband, John and her 3 children, have 4 rescue dogs in their family. She often says she has 7 children because her family treats each of their 'fur-babies' as one of their own. .

Alexandria Prince - Co-Founder/Vice President

Alexandria is a co-founder of this school and is responsible for keeping the curriculum updated as the trends in the industry change. She received her Certification in Dog Training (CDT) from Catch Canine Trainers Academy in 2018. She is married to her husband, Adam, and together, they have 5 kids (Alicia, Tommy, Adam Jr.,Kiley and Jill) and 2 rescue dogs, Ellie and Sam.



Bruce Armstrong - Chief Financial Officer

Bruce joined our school as the CFO when it first opened. He brings with him a career in the Financial Industry. He graduated from the University of Central Missouri with a Masters degree in Business Adminstration in 2010. He is currently with his long-time partner, Sara, with whom he has 2 kids, Amber and William. They also foster dogs for the local animal shelter.
